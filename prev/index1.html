Summary

I design efficient, provably correct distributed algorithms and like to bridge theory and practice. My work centers on synchronization in shared-memory models and the role of randomization in making concurrent systems simpler and faster.

Previously: BSc in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology; head TA and TA for programming paradigms, theory, randomized algorithms, and data structures.
